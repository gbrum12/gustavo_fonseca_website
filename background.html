My Background:

My entire family is from Brazil. We are from Rio de Janeiro, the city with that big statue of Christ and the nicest beaches.
The culture down there is amazing, it feels like everyone is a part of one big family. And that also applies to my own family.
My dad's side of the family is portuguese and native brazilian. My mom's side is mixed, my middle name is german, and I
have no clue where that came from. Colonization in Brazil was a mess, so it's a little complicated to not be related to many races and nationalities.

I lived in Rio for about 16 years, my life was probably what you would imagine if you have heard of Rio before.
I would go to the beach every week, I would go to the pool, play soccer, basketball, beach volleyball, and get acai.
I made a lot of friendships that will last forever, and leaving them was definitely the hardest part of coming here.
But with their support, basketball, and having my family, I am managing to not go crazy.
